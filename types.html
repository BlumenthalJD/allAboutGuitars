Acoustic Guitars:

These guitars have a hollow body and are steel string or nylon string.

Electric Guitars:

These guitars have a solid body and are plugged into an amplifier to make them louder and can add effects.

Twelve String Guitars:

These guitars have a hollow or solid body and can be plugged in or played without depending on the guitar. They have twice as many strings to make the strums contain more harmony.
